The Multi is a student-run design conference in the Spring hosted at Purchase College!

We bring in a group of artists and designers to come speak to the students/showcase their work. The conference runs for 4 days in April and hosts events and workshops that will run alongside the lectures.

The goal is to create an engaging conversation between students and working creatives on process, the 'real world', and other topics pertaining to the artist.
